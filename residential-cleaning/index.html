Our Cleaning Checklist

When we clean your home, we ensure no corner is left untouched. Our thorough cleaning process includes:

  • Dusting all flat surfaces including furniture, shelves, and decor
  • Cleaning kitchen appliances inside and out (microwave, oven, refrigerator)
  • Mopping and vacuuming all floors with attention to corners and edges
  • Unloading dishwasher and putting away clean dishes
  • Deep cleaning bathrooms including showers, tubs, toilets, and sinks
  • Bedroom cleaning with bed making and organization
  • Optional laundry services available upon request
